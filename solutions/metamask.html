Please keep in mind that this process is not equivalent to importing an account using a private key or private key JSON file.

⚠️ WARNING: Importing a Secret Recovery Phrase into an instance of MetaMask will ERASE all existing MetaMask data.

Restore Secret Recovery Phrase to a new MetaMask install

1. During setup from a fresh install of MetaMask, click 'Import Wallet'. On Mobile, this will read 'Import using Secret Recovery Phrase'.

2. Enter your Secret Recovery Phrase and follow the prompts.

In an existing MetaMask install, restore from the lock screen:

1.Select 'Lock' in the top-right menu

2. Click 'Import using Secret Recovery Phrase'

3. Enter your 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase (all lower case, with spaces, and in the correct order) and a new password.

4. Click 'Restore'.

1.Lock the wallet app by tapping the hamburger icon in the top left of the app, and then 'Lock' at the bottom:

2. Look for the 'Reset wallet' link at the bottom of the Unlock screen, and tap it to proceed.

3.A warning message will show up. Make sure you're aware of the implications of resetting your wallet. Remember that if you don't have the correct Secret Recovery Phrase available, you won't be able to access your account again.

4.Confirm you want to go ahead by typing the word 'delete' into the text field that appears after tapping 'I understand, continue'.

5.Now your MetaMask app has essentially returned to the state it was in when you first downloaded it. On the 'Wallet setup' screen, tap 'Import using Secret Recovery Phrase':

6.Now input your Secret Recovery Phrase, all lower case, in the correct order, and including spaces between words. Also, choose a new password.

7.If everything is ok, by tapping 'Import' you should be redirected to the confirmation page shown below, and you'll be able to access your wallet as normal.

To backup your MetaMask Secret Recovery Phrase follow these steps:

1. Open MetaMask and click on the main menu at the top right.

2. Click "Settings" and then click "Security & Privacy".

3. Click on "Reveal Secret Security Phrase".

4. Enter your MetaMask wallet password and then click "Next".

5. Click "Copy to clipboard" or "Save as CSV File".

NOTE: Always remember to backup the recovery phrase and save it in a safe place. Never share it with anyone.
