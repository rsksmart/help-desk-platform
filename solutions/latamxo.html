LatamXO is a White Label non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet for Android, iOS, and web. It is a market-ready solution with more than 500 tokens on 6 supported networks.

LatamXO wallet offers these features in one place:

✅ Send and receive crypto.

✅ Transaction history.

✅ Mnemonic phrase for security and privacy.

✅ Create and restore wallets.

Yes. If you have a previous wallet, you will have the option to import it with your recovery phrase. However, it will always be possible to create a new wallet. Always remember to back up the recovery phrase and never share it with anyone.

If you are using a fresh installed instance of LatamXO, the app will create a new wallet automatically. However, there is the option to import a previous one.

In case you already have a wallet, you have to follow these steps:

1) Open LatamXO app and tap on the gear icon located at the top right.

2) Tap on "Seed and then tap on "Import".

3) Manually type the list of words of your seed. Remember to respect the order of the words.

4) Once you are done, click the "Import" button. If everything is correct, you will see the balances of your imported account.

To backup the Seed Phrase (also known as Recovery phrase or Security phrase) of your address in LatamXO wallet follow these steps:

1) Open LatamXO app and tap on the gear icon located at the top right.

2) Tap on "Seed" and then tap on "Seed Phrase".

3) The recovery phrase will be masked. Tap on "Show words" to reveal the words.

4) Manually type the list of words to make a backup copy. Remember to respect the order of the words.

NOTE: Save the recovery phrase in a safe place, and do not share them with anyone.

To connect your LatamXO wallet with dapps in the Rootstock network (e.g. RSK Token Bridge, Sovryn, Money On Chain, etc) you will need to use WalletConnect, which is an open protocol for connecting Dapps to mobile Wallets using end-to-end encryption by scanning a QR code. For more information regarding this protocol, you can visit the official WalletConnect page.

LatamXO does not charge fees for transactions with its wallet. However, some services like swapping usually charge a fee. Additionally, users will need to pay transaction cost fees (gas), which are charged by the network. In each blockchain there is a particular currency with which the transaction cost is paid, the native currency of that network. On the Ethereum network it is ETH, on the Rootstock network it is RBTC, on Binance Smart Chain it is BNB, etc.
