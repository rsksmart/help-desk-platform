Quidli is a web app designed for groups to share crypto as micro-rewards with top collaborators and community members through Web 2.0 tools such as browsers, email, Slack and Discord.

Quidli is quite simple. Once you have created your Quidli account, you need to buy/deposit crypto to top up your account. This can be done from the integrated Moonpay ramp service or via a deposit from an external wallet.

Once your Quidli account has a balance, you will be able to send crypto to group members directly from your browser, via email, and in Slack or Discord.

Note: Quidli takes custody of the crypto you use in the application.

Quidli can be used as a standalone web app (send crypto rewards from your browser and through email) and or be integrated into online collaboration tools like Slack and Discord.

Transactions on Quidli are off-chain. This means that you and your group members are not subject to blockchain fees (gas) for internal transactions.

However, you still have to pay gas for deposits you make into your account, while gas for withdrawals is currently subsidized by Quidli.

Currently, Quidli supports the following coins/tokens: BTC (Bitcoin), RBTC (Rootstock), ETH (Ethereum), DAI (ERC20), USDC (ERC20), BUSD (Binance), among many others.
